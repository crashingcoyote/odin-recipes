Pasta

Description

Pasta is a staple food in Italian cuisine that is made from unleavened dough consisting of wheat flour and water, or sometimes eggs. The dough is typically rolled out into various shapes, such as spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne, and then boiled in salted water until it is cooked to the desired texture. Pasta is often served with a sauce, such as marinara, Alfredo, or pesto, and can also be used as a base for other dishes, such as lasagna or baked ziti. Pasta is a popular and versatile food that is enjoyed around the world.
