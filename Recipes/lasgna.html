Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta noodles, meat or vegetable-based sauce, and cheese. The dish is typically baked in the oven, allowing the flavors of the ingredients to meld together and create a deliciously comforting meal. Lasagna can be made in various ways, with different types of pasta, sauces, and fillings, making it a versatile and customizable dish that can be enjoyed by many.
